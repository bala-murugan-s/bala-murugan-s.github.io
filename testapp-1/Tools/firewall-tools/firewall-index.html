Notes & Roadmap

Where this fits in your workflow

The idea is to keep frequently used CLI and patterns just a click away — especially during incident response, change windows, and maintenance activity.

  • Standardize troubleshooting flows across engineers and shifts.
  • Reduce copy‑paste from old notes, PDFs, or vendor docs.
  • Provide a single place to evolve CLI “runbooks” over time.

Additional vendors (Fortinet, Check Point, Cisco, etc.) can be added here as dedicated modules once their CLI sets and flows are curated.
